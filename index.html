What is Scrum?

Scrum is a way for teams to work on a project or product. The Scrum methodology enables for teams to come up with an idea of how to work on a project, try it out, reflect on the experience, and make appropriate adjustments. In this way, Scrum encourages teams to learn through experiences and continuously improve their work. Watch this 5 min Youtube video to get some basic information about Scrum and how it is used.

Events and roles in Scrum

Scrum is a kind of framework what includes different roles and events. The events in Scrum are called "sprints". The different sprints have certain purposes to dynamically try and improve ideas in a project. Here you can read more about the events in Scrum.

The roles in Scrum are:

  • The product owner
  • The Scrum master
  • The development team
